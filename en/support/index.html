Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between a category and a group?

The category says what it is (warranty, invoice, document, check). The group says who or what it belongs to (Home, Pool, Personal, Kids…). You can filter by either, or both.

How are warranty due dates computed?

Enter the purchase date and the warranty duration (6, 12, 24 months…): Échéo computes the expiry date. You can also enter an expiry date directly (handy for a document or a contract).

When do I get reminders?

You choose reminders per item: 30 days before, 7 days before, the day itself… They arrive in the morning. Everything is local; no data is sent.

Is my data synced?

Yes, via your private iCloud if iCloud is enabled. Your data stays on your devices; it never goes through our servers.

What's included in Échéo Premium?

Unlimited items, unlimited attachments, custom reminders, PDF inventory export, iCloud sync and all themes. Monthly or yearly subscription, or a one-time "lifetime" purchase. Cancel anytime in your Apple account settings.

How do I delete my data?

Delete the app (local data is erased) and, if needed, remove Échéo from iCloud in Settings → iCloud.
